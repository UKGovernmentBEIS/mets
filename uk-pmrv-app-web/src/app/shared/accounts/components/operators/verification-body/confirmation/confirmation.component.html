What happens next

Your appointed verifier will be able to review your activities within your permit account in preparation for verification of your Annual Emissions Monitoring (AEM) report.

The primary contact on your account will receive an email on Jan 1st each year giving notice that a task has appeared on the permit account dashboard to produce and submit an AEM report by March 31st.

When the AEM report has been submitted the verifier will receive a task on their dashboard to verify the AEM report.

An operator admin user on your account can change verifiers by selecting the users and contacts option from the dashboard.
