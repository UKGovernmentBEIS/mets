You must check that the monitoring approach (and specific details) and the sampling plan have been correctly applied and implemented.

If CO2 is transferred and measured by both the transferring and receiving installation, you need to check if:

  • differences between the measured values at both installations can be explained by the uncertainty of the measurement systems
  • the correct arithmetic average of the measured values has been used in the emission reports of both installations

If the differences cannot be explained, you must check if:

  • adjustments were made to align the differences between the measured values
  • those adjustments were conservative
  • the competent authority has granted approval for those adjustments
