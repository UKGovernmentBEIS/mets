You must check that:

  • the methods used for completing any missing data in the monitoring plan were appropriate for the specific situation, and that the operator has applied them correctly
  • the control activities which the operator has used to prevent missing data are effective

If the operator has approval to use other methods, you must check that they have applied and documented them correctly.

If the operator cannot get approval from the competent authority in time, you must check that their approach to completing the missing data does not underestimate the emissions, and lead to material misstatements.
