If the operator needs to comply with the uncertainty thresholds for activity data and calculation factors, you must confirm that they have used valid information used in the monitoring plan.

If they have not used a tier-based approach you must check that they have:

  • assessed and quantified the uncertainty
  • met the required overall uncertainty threshold for the annual level of GHG emissions
  • used valid information in the monitoring plan
  • used an overall approach in line with Article 22(b) of Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) 2018/2066
  • provided evidence to show they have met the conditions for the fall-back approach used

If the operator needs to make a simplified uncertainty assessment, you need to confirm that they are using valid information.
