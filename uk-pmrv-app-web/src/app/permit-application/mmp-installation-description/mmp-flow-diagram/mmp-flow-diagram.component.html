The diagram must include:

  • the technical elements of the installation, identifying emission sources and heat-producing and consuming units
  • all energy and material flows, including source streams, emission sources, measurable and non-measurable heat flows, relevant electricity flows and waste gases
  • the points of measurement and metering devices
  • boundaries of the sub-installations, indicating the split between sub-installations serving sectors at risk of carbon leakage and those serving other sectors, based on NACE rev. 2 or PRODCOM 2010

In complex cases a more detailed flow diagram should be uploaded for each relevant sub-installation.
