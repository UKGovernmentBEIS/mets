Based on the information you gave, this installation can apply for a Hospital and Small Emitter (HSE) permit or a full Greenhouse Gas Emissions (GHGE) permit.

Ultra small emitters

You are an ultra small emitter if your installation emits less than 2,500 tonnes of carbon dioxide equivalent (CO2eq) in the relevant period.

Applying for an HSE permit

You can only apply for an HSE permit if you have HSE status for the relevant allocation period.

Applying for a GHGE permit

Installations which carry out regulated activities and are not eligible for HSE status must have a GHGE permit.
