You must apply for an emissions monitoring plan within 42 days of becoming a UK ETS aircraft operator.

This is when you have carried out an aviation activity in Schedule 1 of the UK ETS Order and:

For commercial aircraft operators (both points apply)

  • In a calendar year you operated 243 or more UK ETS full-scope flights in any 4-month period (January to April, May to August, September to December)
  • Your emissions from UK ETS full-scope flights are 10,000 or more tonnes of CO2

For non-commercial aircraft operators

  • Your annual emissions from UK ETS full-scope flights are 1000 or more tonnes of CO2
