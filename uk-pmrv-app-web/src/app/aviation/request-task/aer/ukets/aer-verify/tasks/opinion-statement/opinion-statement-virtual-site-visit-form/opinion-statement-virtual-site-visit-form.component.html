Reasons for making a virtual visit

Where the aircraft operator is a small emitter, you must state:

  • the result of the risk analysis which meant a virtual verification could take place

Where the aircraft operator is not a small emitter, you must state:

  • the result of the risk analysis which meant a virtual verification could take place
  • how the visit and verification were made from a technical perspective
  • the measures taken to reduce the verification risk to an acceptable level
  • whether a generic authorisation for the visit was issued in accordance with Article 34b of the Accreditation and Verification Regulations
