Confirmation of no double-counting

Do all of the batch claims reviewed contain evidence that shows the sustainability, purchase and delivery criteria were met?

Valid batch claims must:

  • meet the RTFO Schedule 1 sustainability criteria
  • be purchased in the scheme year, or up to three months beforehand
  • be delivered no later than 31 March of the year after the scheme year
