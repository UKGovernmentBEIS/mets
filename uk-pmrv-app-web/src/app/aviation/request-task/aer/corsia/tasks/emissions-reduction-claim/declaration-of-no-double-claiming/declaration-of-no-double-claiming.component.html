You must upload a copy (signed by an authorised person) of a document titled ‘Declaration of no double counting’ on company headed paper.

This must state that no CEF included in your claim has been:

  • claimed by this aeroplane operator under any other schemes it has participated in during the current compliance period and the compliance period immediately preceding it
  • sold by the aeroplane operator to someone else

Your regulator can provide you with an example of the declaration content.
