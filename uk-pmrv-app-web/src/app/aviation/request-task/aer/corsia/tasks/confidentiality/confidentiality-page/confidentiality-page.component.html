Each year ICAO publishes:

  • total annual CO2 emissions aggregated for all aeroplane operators on each state pair (identifying state pairs with offsetting obligations)
  • for each aeroplane operator: total annual emissions on all international routes and total annual emissions on routes with or without offsetting obligations

Operators can request that certain data at the state pair or operator level is not published by ICAO, but your regulator will determine if this data should be confidential or not.

Your total annual emissions data

If you operate a very limited number of flights between state pairs you can ask ICAO not to publish your total annual emissions data at the aeroplane operator level, as stated in article 31(5) of the Air Navigation Order.
