Request two-factor authentication reset

If you have lost your device and cannot access your authenticator, you must request a reset from your regulator or administrator.

Operators

Contact your regulator or an operator administrator.

Regulators

Contact your regulator administrator.

Verifiers

Contact your regulator or verifier administrator.

You can contact your regulator or administrator by phone or by email. If you are sending an email, use the subject line 'I have lost access to my authenticator app - Manage your UK ETS reporting', and include the name of the account or verification body you belong to.
